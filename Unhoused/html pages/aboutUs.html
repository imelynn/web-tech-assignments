About Us


Established in 2022 by Feta Group our company saw a need to help unhoused individuals in a way that served them best. We asked them how we can help and they told us what they needed.

"I want to receive help anonymously. Within that discretion I need to understand the resources available to me and choose what suits my needs."

Unhoused focuses on providing access to the right resources, in the right way, to the right individuals affected by homelessness. We strive to deliver resource information in the most efficient and useful way. We work diligently to ensure that we are providing up to date information relevant to the users current needs.

We also understood there was need and desire for individuals not affected by homelessness to help in any way they can. We also asked them how can we make it efficient for them to help fight the of homelessness and they said:

"The situation that I find myself in makes me feel helpless as I cannot quit my job to help. I feel that if there was a way to contribute to finding a solution to homelessness in an easier manner, I would help all the time."
