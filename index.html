Feminine Energy in Tech Always

FETA, presented by E. Lynn, is an ideology that more feminine energy is needed in the METAverse. Be it real, be it metaphysical, feminine energy is a distinct area of importance in the world of Tech. Not only is it a belief that addresses the marginalization of women in tech, but also a very under represented demographic within that population, Women of Color.

For many reasons female energy is necessary, at the very least it encompasses the structure of cycles. Cycles incorporate balance into life as we know it. Just like nature, technology has a life cycle in and of itself.

Feminine energy is a sacred creation that provides the balance needed for masculine energy to flourish in ALL Aspects of Life.

This site exists to showcase that women in tech are a vital necessity. If you are interested in learning more please navigate around the site and explore some of the things that Women in Tech are doing.


Recognize and embrace your uniqueness...Being a Black woman, being a woman in general, on a team of all men, means that you are going to have a unique voice.

It’s important to embrace that.
